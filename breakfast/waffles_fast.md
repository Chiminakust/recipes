# Ingredients

- all purpose flour, 2 cups
- milk, 1 3/4 cups
- baking powder, 4 tsp
- egg, 2
- sugar, 3 tbs
- neutral oil, 1/2 cup
- salt, 1/4 tsp
- vanilla extract, 1/2 tsp

# Preparation

- Mix the dry ingredients together.
- Mix the wet ingredients together.
- Form a well in the dry mix and pour in the wet mix.
- Mix to combine.
- Cook for 3-5 minutes
