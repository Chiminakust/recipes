# Ingredients

- 4 links italian sausage
- 2 tbs olive oil
- 1 large onion (yellow or red), diced
- 2 red bell peppers, sliced
- 1 can coconut milk
- 2 to 3 tbs indian curry paste
- 1 tsp brown sugar
- 1 / 2 tsp salt
- 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinced
- black pepper
- rice for serving
- chopped cilantro or parsley for garnish
