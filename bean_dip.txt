Ingredients:

1 onion
1 can black beans, drained
1 tps cumin
vegetable oil
hot sauce
cheese, in cubes
salt
pepper
water

Preparation:

- Dice the onion
- sauté the onion in the pan with some oil
- when the onion starts to be cooked, add the beans
- when the beans start to cook, add everything else to taste except the cheese
- when everything is cooked together, add the cheese to melt
- serve as is or throw in a pie pan with cheese on top, then in the oven at 300°f to melt the cheese
